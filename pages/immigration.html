Immigration Services

Guiding you through complex immigration paperwork with trusted, personalized support.

How We Help with Immigration

At FlexPoint Services, we assist you in preparing and submitting USCIS forms—without offering legal advice. Whether you need a fiancé(e) visa, a family‑based petition, or help with naturalization, we guide you step‑by‑step through the process.

Forms We Can Help With

  • Fiancé(e) Visa (Form I‑129F)
  • Family‑Sponsored Petition (Form I‑130)
  • Green Card Renewal (Form I‑90)
  • Adjustment of Status (Form I‑485)
  • Naturalization (Form N‑400)
  • Affidavit of Support (Form I‑864)
  • Travel Permits, Document Translations, and more

We’ll review your documents, flag any missing information, and assemble your package so you file accurately and on time.

Appointments are available after‑hours and on weekends for your convenience.
