How We Help with Your Taxes

At FlexPoint Services, we take the stress out of tax season by offering expert tax preparation for both individuals and small businesses. Our approach is flexible and client-centered — whether you're a salaried employee, gig worker, freelancer, or a small business owner, we’ll ensure your taxes are filed accurately and on time.

We’ll review your documents, uncover every possible deduction, and help you minimize your liability. If you're self-employed or operate a business, we handle quarterly filings, Schedule C, and more. We also assist with prior-year corrections, ITIN renewals, and IRS correspondence.

Enjoy in-person appointments, remote support, and extended hours including weekends. We’re here to make sure you file confidently.
